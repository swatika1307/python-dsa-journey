## Section 02 - Sorting II

- Problems:
1. Merge sort
2. Recursive bubble sort

- Patterns learned:
1. Divide & Conquer: Break a problem into smaller subproblems, solve them recursively, and combine the results (Merge Sort)
2. Merge Technique: Merge two already sorted arrays into one sorted array
3. Recursive Thinking: Convert iterative sorting into recursive implementation without changing the core logic
